La Fortuna

Come and explore the beauty of La Fortuna, Costa Rica!

The city's natural beauty

La Fortuna is a city that is located in Costa Rica. The City is surrounded in a tropical region that includes rivers, forests, and the famous Volcano Arenal!

Delicious food

While exploring La Fortuna, you can excite your taste buds with the delicious traditional foods that all of Costa Rica has to offer. Come and enjoy all the great food!

Try out the local activities

Here at La Fortuna, there are many activities that are available to make your visit much more exctiting, thrilling, enjoyable, and memorable.

Such activities include but are not limited to:

  • Ziplining
  • ATV adventures
  • Horseback riding
  • Observing sloths at a sloth park
  • Tours

About

La Fortuna, which is also known as "City of fortune", is a city located in the northwest part of Costa Rica. The city is a very popular attraction for tourists because of the marvelous scenery of the region. Upon arriving at La Fortuna, many people have the chance to explore its beauty, enjoy some of the local activities available, and some of the great food that are made by the locals. It truly is a place full of rich history and fortune!
